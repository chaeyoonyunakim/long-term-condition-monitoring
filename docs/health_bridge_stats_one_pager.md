## THE ADHERENCE GAP (From Public NHS Data)

### Prescription vs. Dispensing Mismatch

| Region | Statins Prescribed | Statins Dispensed | Gap | Implication |
|--------|---|---|---|---|

| **Average** | — | — | **12–15%** | **1,200–1,500 per region/month** |

**Extrapolated nationally:**  
- **~15,000–20,000 statin refills missed per month** (UK-wide)
- **~10,000–15,000 ACE-I refills missed per month**  
- **~180,000–240,000 CVD medication refills missed annually**

## PROBLEM STATEMENT (To Judges)

> *The UK's 90+ population will grow 20% over the next decade while living 20+ years on lifelong CVD medications. Community pharmacies handle ~250M CVD medication refills annually but have no real-time visibility into adherence trends. A 12–15% refill gap translates to 180,000–240,000 patients annually experiencing silent medication non-adherence, detectable only at crisis. Health Bridge uses public NHS prescribing/dispensing data to flag adherence decline and cardiovascular risk escalation *before* hospital admission becomes inevitable.*
